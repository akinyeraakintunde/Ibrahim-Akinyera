NxtAbroad AI and Digital Transformation

Evidence document – Tech Nation Global Talent (Digital Technology)
Prepared by: Ibrahim Akinyera (Founder and Managing Director, NxtAbroad Limited)

1. Overview

NxtAbroad Limited is an education and travel consultancy that supports students and clients with study abroad, visa and migration processes. As Founder and Managing Director, I have led the digital transformation of NxtAbroad, implementing systems, processes and data practices that move the business from manual operations to a scalable, technology-enabled platform.

2. Challenges Addressed

As the company grew, NxtAbroad faced operational bottlenecks:

  • Fragmented client information and manual tracking.
  • Limited visibility into application stages and pipeline health.
  • Inconsistent follow-up and communication patterns.
  • Difficulty in extracting data for decision-making and marketing.

My goal was to redesign the operating model using digital tools, structured data and automation, creating a foundation that can later support AI-assisted decision-making and scoring for applicants.

3. Key Initiatives and Systems

3.1 Process Mapping and CRM Foundations

I mapped the end-to-end NxtAbroad client journey, from initial enquiry through consultation, application, visa decision and travel. Based on this, I introduced structured tracking of:

  • Client profiles and communication history.
  • Application stages and document readiness.
  • University and country preferences.
  • Conversion rates and pipeline metrics.

3.2 Templates, Automation and Data Capture

I designed and implemented:

  • Template documents for offers, visa letters and sponsorship evidence.
  • Operational checklists and standard operating procedures for staff.
  • Digital intake forms to capture consistent data at the point of lead generation.

These assets reduce error, improve service quality and capture structured data that can be used by future analytics and AI components.

3.3 Future AI and Scoring Capability

The systems I designed are intentionally structured to enable future AI use cases, including:

  • Applicant scoring models (likelihood of visa success or offer acceptance).
  • Recommendation systems for course and university matching.
  • Automated follow-up and communication triggers.

4. My Role and Leadership

As Founder and Managing Director, my contributions are both strategic and technical:

  • Designing the process architecture for NxtAbroad’s operations.
  • Defining data structures for tracking client journeys.
  • Creating documentation, templates and operational policies.
  • Embedding basic digital tools that can later be extended with AI.

5. Relevance to Tech Nation

This initiative shows my ability to:

  • Lead digital transformation in a real business.
  • Build systems and processes that are technically informed.
  • Lay the groundwork for AI-enhanced services in education and migration.
