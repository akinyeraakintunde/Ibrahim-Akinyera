Introduction

My name is Ibrahim Akintunde Akinyera, and I am applying for endorsement under the Exceptional Promise route of the UK Global Talent Visa in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity Engineering. My background combines machine learning engineering, security analytics, digital operations, and applied data science. I have lived, studied, and worked in the United Kingdom, and I have contributed to independent projects and industry activities that align strongly with the UK’s digital priorities.

I am committed to building a long-term engineering career in the UK. My objective is to contribute to secure machine learning systems, advanced cybersecurity infrastructure, and technology initiatives that protect digital environments across industries.

Career Background and Technical Journey

My academic foundation is built on a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Fountain University, Nigeria and a Master’s degree in Data Science from Ulster University, Belfast. During my postgraduate studies, I developed advanced skills in supervised learning, predictive modelling, big data processing, and cybersecurity analytics. These experiences introduced me to the intersection of machine learning and security, where I saw the need for intelligent systems capable of detecting, preventing, and mitigating digital threats.

After my studies, I continued to build complex machine learning projects with a strong focus on network security, risk analysis, and real-time detection. My work includes building ML models, designing data pipelines, implementing FastAPI backends, and developing dashboards for live intrusion predictions.

Key Technical Contributions

1. Machine Learning–Based Intrusion Detection System (IDS)

I developed a complete Intrusion Detection System using the NSL-KDD dataset, capable of classifying network traffic into normal or attack categories. The project includes data preprocessing, feature engineering, a Random Forest classifier, ROC–AUC evaluation (AUC = 0.961), a saved model artefact, a FastAPI service with OpenAPI documentation, and a dashboard for real-time inference. It demonstrates end-to-end ML and cybersecurity engineering aligned with production standards.

2. Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus – Connected Health (UK)

I contributed to the Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus (CE+) certification process at Connected Health, a major digital health provider in Northern Ireland and the UK. My work involved endpoint configuration and validation, secure configuration checks, firewall and antivirus verification, patch management and vulnerability compliance, and preparing technical evidence for independent assessors. This strengthened the organisation’s operational security posture and supported successful CE+ certification.

3. Independent Research and Engineering Projects

I have developed additional systems including a Twitter topic classifier, a risk-scoring engine, automated data pipelines, and API-ready ML deployments. These projects demonstrate consistent growth, technical independence, and strong potential as an emerging engineering leader.

Long-Term Plan in the United Kingdom

My long-term goal is to build a career in the United Kingdom as a Senior Machine Learning and Cybersecurity Engineer. I plan to:

  • Short term (0–2 years): Join a UK-based tech company and contribute to secure ML systems, cyber analytics, and intrusion detection products.
  • Medium term (3–5 years): Progress into senior roles leading ML security initiatives, architecting end-to-end pipelines, and delivering high-impact engineering work.
  • Long term (5+ years): Become a recognised contributor to the UK’s digital security landscape, working on large-scale ML infrastructure, threat detection platforms, and mentoring junior engineers.

Why the UK and Why Exceptional Promise

The UK has a world-class ecosystem for AI, cybersecurity, and digital innovation. Standards such as Cyber Essentials Plus, and organisations including NCSC and NHS Digital, provide the right environment for my technical development. My evidence demonstrates real contributions in cybersecurity implementation and advanced ML engineering, clear potential for leadership, and alignment with UK priorities.

I respectfully request endorsement under the Exceptional Promise route and I am committed to contributing to secure AI and cybersecurity innovation in the United Kingdom.
